Code Quiz Game

Introduction:

You will be taking a timed quiz on JavaScript fundamentals. It will store high scores and will be compared to your other classmates.

Instructions:

When you click the START button, a timer with 75 seconds will begin and you will be presented with a question. If you answer the question incorrectly, time will be subctracted from the clock. When all the questions are answered or the timer reaches to ZERO "0" the game is over and you will be given a slot to save your initials. Have fun!
